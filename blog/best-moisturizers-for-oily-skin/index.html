Why Oily Skin Needs Moisturizer

Your skin has two separate hydration systems:

Oil (sebum): Produced by sebaceous glands, creates a protective lipid layer. Oily skin overproduces this.

How to Moisturize Oily Skin

Morning Routine

  1. Cleanse with a gentle foaming cleanser
  2. Apply toner (optional ‚Äî skip if it feels like too many steps)
  3. Apply a thin layer of gel or lotion moisturizer to damp skin
  4. Wait 1-2 minutes
  5. Apply sunscreen (SPF 30+)

Evening Routine

  1. Double cleanse (oil cleanser to remove sunscreen, then foaming cleanser)
  2. Apply active treatments (retinol, niacinamide serum, etc.)
  3. Wait 5-10 minutes
  4. Apply moisturizer
